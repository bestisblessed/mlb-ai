Park Factors

<< 6/11 June 12, 2025 6/13 >>

Last Updated: 7:37 AM

Thursday's slate of seven outdoor games features a mixed bag of temperatures, with 90° expected at three of the venues and 60s forecasted at three others. Giants / Rockies is among the hot games with a dry 90° at Coors for the afternoon start. The other two scorchers are Camden Yards (DET/BAL) and Citi Field (WAS/NYM). All else being equal, higher temperatures are better for offense as the ball flies farther when it's warmer.

Wrigley Field is among the cooler venues on Thursday where it will be in the mid 60s for Pirates / Cubs. While the wind is only forecasted to blow in the single digits, the in-blowing direction is significant as even light winds can have an impact at Wrigley.

It will also be in the 60s in Milwaukee where the roof is unofficially expected to be open.
