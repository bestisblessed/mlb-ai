Gio Urshela Bio

  • Fullname: Giovanny Urshela
  • Born: 10/11/1991 in Cartagena, Colombia
  • Debut: 6/09/2015

Giovanny Urshela
Giovanny Urshela...is married, Danna...has a daughter, Gianna...in February 2021, was named an official Health Ambassador for Hackensack Meridian Health...in the role, engaged with pediatric patients through virtual visits and other events at the Joseph M. Sanzari Children’s Hospital in Hackensack, K. Hovananian Children’s Hospital in Neptune, N.J., and other locations throughout the network.

2024

Began the season with Detroit, was released in mid-August, and signed with Atlanta...combined with both clubs to bat .250 with nine home runs and 52 RBI in 128 games in his ninth season in the Majors...His batting average, on-base percentage (.286), slugging percentage (.361) and OPS (.647) were all his lowest over the last six years...had the third most games played of his career...Batted .271 (91-for-336) with all nine of his home runs and a .701 OPS against right-handed pitchers compared to .177 (17-for-96) with a .462 OPS against left-handers...his career splits are .272 with a .728 OPS against righties, .275 with a .732 OPS against lefties...Hit .308 with five home runs and 26 RBI in 51 day games, .213 with four home runs and 26 RBI in 77 night games...Batted .419 in the ninth inning and was 5-for-10 (.500) in extra innings...his five extra inning hits tied for the most in the Majors...is a .364 (16-for-44) career hitter in extra innings...Started 102 games at third base and 15 at first base...also appeared in one game each at shortstop and designated hitter...committed seven errors, all as a third baseman, for a .972 fielding percentage...ranked seventh among Major League third basemen in fielding and ninth in double plays (22)...Was 0-for-5 with a walk as a pinch hitter...Started games in each of the bottom six spots in the order, including 31 in the eight spot and 28 batting fifth...hit .353 in 10 starts batting ninth.
