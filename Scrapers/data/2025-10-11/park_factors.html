The roof at American Family Field will be closed for Game 5 with high 50s in Milwaukee. When closed, the venue plays slightly favorable for home runs and below the league average for both runs and in-play hits. While the shallow center-field third is a good target for homers, the close gaps make the outfield easier to cover.
