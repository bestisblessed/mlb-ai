J.D. Martinez played 14 seasons.

Is J.D. Martinez in the Hall of Fame?

J.D. Martinez has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was J.D. Martinez drafted?

J.D. Martinez was drafted by the Minnesota Twins in the 36th round of the 2006 MLB June Amateur Draft from Charles W. Flanagan HS (Pembroke Pines, FL) and the Houston Astros in the 20th round of the 2009 MLB June Amateur Draft from Nova Southeastern University (Fort Lauderdale, FL).

J.D. Martinez was an Outfielder and Designated Hitter.
