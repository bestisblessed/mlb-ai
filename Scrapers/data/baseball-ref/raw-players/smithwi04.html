Will Smith played 12 seasons.

Is Will Smith in the Hall of Fame?

Will Smith has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Will Smith drafted?

Will Smith was drafted by the Tampa Bay Devil Rays in the 40th round of the 2007 MLB June Amateur Draft from Northgate HS (Newnan, GA) and the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim in the 7th round of the 2008 MLB June Amateur Draft from Gulf Coast State College (Panama City, FL).

Will Smith was a Pitcher.

Will Smith had 748 strikeouts over his career.

Will Smith played for 6 teams; the Kansas City Royals, Milwaukee Brewers, San Francisco Giants, Atlanta Braves, Houston Astros and Texas Rangers.
