Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Bryce Harper?

Bryce Harper is 32 years old.

When was Bryce Harper born?

Bryce Harper was born on October 16, 1992.

Where was Bryce Harper born?

Bryce Harper was born in Las Vegas, NV.

How tall is Bryce Harper?

Bryce Harper is 6-2 (188 cm) tall.

How much does Bryce Harper weigh?

Bryce Harper weighs 210 lbs (95 kg).

How many seasons has Bryce Harper played?

Bryce Harper is in his 14th season.

When was Bryce Harper drafted?

Bryce Harper was drafted by the Washington Nationals in the 1st round (1st) of the 2010 MLB June Amateur Draft from College of Southern Nevada (Henderson, NV).

What position does Bryce Harper play?

Bryce Harper is a Rightfielder and First Baseman.

How many hits does Bryce Harper have?

Bryce Harper has 19 hits this season and has 1,689 hits over his career.

How many home runs does Bryce Harper have?

Bryce Harper has 5 home runs this season and has 341 home runs over his career.

What is Bryce Harper's average?

Bryce Harper has a .264 average this season and has a .281 average over his career.

How much is Bryce Harper worth?

Bryce Harper makes $27,538,462.00 this year. His current contract terms are 13 yrs/$330M (19-31).

How many teams has Bryce Harper played for?

Bryce Harper has played for 2 teams; the Washington Nationals and Philadelphia Phillies.

Bryce Harper has never won a World Series.
