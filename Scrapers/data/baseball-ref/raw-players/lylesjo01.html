How old is Jordan Lyles?

Jordan Lyles is 34 years old.

Jordan Lyles was born on October 19, 1990.

Jordan Lyles weighed 230 lbs (104 kg) when playing.

Jordan Lyles played 14 seasons.

Is Jordan Lyles in the Hall of Fame?

Jordan Lyles has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Jordan Lyles drafted?

Jordan Lyles was drafted by the Houston Astros in the 1st round (38th) of the 2008 MLB June Amateur Draft from Hartsville HS (Hartsville, SC).

Jordan Lyles was a Pitcher.

Jordan Lyles had 1,145 strikeouts over his career.
